# Personas (starter)

## 1) Loop-based Producer
- wants speed and inspiration
- hates theory-heavy UIs
- needs quick usable harmony

## 2) Keys Player / Performer
- wants mapping, splits, and expressive triggering
- cares about feel, dynamics, transitions

## 3) Songwriter
- wants chords that *sound finished*
- needs voicing + range control
