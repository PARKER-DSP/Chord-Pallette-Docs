## Success Metrics

**Prelaunch (Phase 1):**
- Email list: 500+
- Demo video views: 10k+
- Beta user satisfaction: 4+/5

**Launch (Phase 3):**
- First week sales: $10k+
- Social reach: 100k+ impressions
- Press mentions: 5+

**Post-Launch (90 days):**
- Active users: 300-500
- Satisfaction: 4.5+/5
- Expansion pack adoption: 20% of user base
