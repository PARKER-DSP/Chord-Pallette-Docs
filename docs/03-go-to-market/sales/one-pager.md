# ChordPallette — One Pager

**ChordPallette** is a performance-driven chord capture and voicing instrument for producers, keys players, and songwriters.

---

## What It Is

A real-time MIDI plugin that captures chord voicings, applies intelligent voice leading, and enables triggering sophisticated chords from simple MIDI input.

**Core workflow:**  
Capture → Modify → Play

---

## Why It Wins

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| Fast chord capture | Capture your best voicings instantly, never lose an idea |
| Non-destructive editing | Try variations without destroying the original |
| Musically smart transitions | Chord-to-chord voice leading that sounds intentional and connected |
| Output range safety | Auto-fit voicings to your instrument; less mud, more musicality |
| Live triggering | Play chord banks like an instrument with expressive control |

---

## Who It's For

- **Producers** – Speed, workflow, inspiration capture
- **Keys Players** – Live performance, expressive triggering
- **Songwriters** – Professional voicings, harmony education
