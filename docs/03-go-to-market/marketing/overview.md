## Feature Descriptions

### Chord Capture
- Capture chords from MIDI, store as triggerable blocks
- From moment of inspiration to organized library instantly

### Voice Leading
- Non-destructive voice leading that connects chords musically in real time
- Style-driven voice leading (Smooth, Cinematic, Neo-Soul, Classical, EDM)
- Your progressions finally flow

### Operators
- Non-destructive operators for voicing, rhythm, humanize
- Transform chords in real time without destroying the original

### Output Control
- Bank-wide output rules to fit your instrument and range
- Less mud, more musicality
- Smart note mapping for any keyboard or pad

### Triggering
- Play your chord bank like an instrument
- MIDI-driven performance with expressive control
- One-note chord triggering with full voicing
