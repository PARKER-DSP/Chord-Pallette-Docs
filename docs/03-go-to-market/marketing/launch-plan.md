# Launch Plan

Phased launch strategy for ChordPallette.

---

## Prelaunch (4–8 weeks before release)

**Goal:** Build anticipation, capture emails, recruit beta users

**Activities:**
- ✅ Landing page + email signup
- ✅ Weekly demo video clips (Twitter/TikTok/YouTube Shorts)
- ✅ Recruit 20–50 beta users
- ✅ Collect testimonials and before/after examples
- ✅ Build affiliate partnerships
- ✅ Create quickstart guide and video walkthrough

**Targets:**
- Email list: 500+
- Demo views: 10k+
- Beta feedback: 3-5 strong testimonials

---

## Launch Week

**Goal:** Maximum visibility and early adoption momentum

**Activities:**
- ✅ Intro pricing window (20-30% discount)
- ✅ Demo video premiere
- ✅ Walkthr ough tutorials
- ✅ Affiliate push (partner codes, promotion)
- ✅ Release notes + quickstart package
- ✅ Press kit distribution

**Targets:**
- First week sales: 100-200 units
- Social media reach: 50k impressions

---

## Post-Launch (Ongoing)

**Goal:** Sustain growth through content and ecosystem

**Phase 3 (Months 2-3):**
- Release operator packs ($9-19 each)
- Publish voice leading tutorials
- Refine pricing based on conversion data
- Expand affiliate network

**Phase 4-5 (Months 4+):**
- Genre-specific voicing packs
- Community chord library / sharing
- Frequent updates + feature releases
- Controller profile marketplace
- AI-assisted features announcement
